The problem

Swiss fundamental data is
fragmented, expensive, or broken.

01

Fragmented across 220 sites

Every listed company publishes PDFs in a different format, in German, French or English. No unified source. No consistent structure.

02

Free tools are unreliable

yfinance breaks multiple times a year. IEX Cloud shut down in 2024 without notice. Alpha Vantage limits to 25 calls/day. No SLA, no guarantee.

03

SPI is a desert for affordable tools

Tikr's free plan covers US only. Stockanalysis has no API and prohibits redistribution. SimFin added European coverage only in late 2023.

04

Enterprise pricing for Swiss data

SIX Financial Information and Bloomberg target large banks — 2,000 CHF/month minimum. No sensibly-priced middle ground exists.

The product

One clean layer for
Swiss equity fundamentals.

⌨

Python API

Native Python package. Clean DataFrames, normalized field names, consistent date ranges. No wrapper around a flaky scraper.

⬡

Web interface

Browse financials for any SIX-listed company. Search, filter, compare. No terminal login, no spreadsheet gymnastics.

↓

Clean exports

CSV and Parquet exports with provenance metadata. Reproducible, versionable, ready for your models without manual cleaning.

Revenue EBITDA EBIT Net Income EPS Free Cash Flow Total Assets Total Equity Total Debt P/E EV/EBITDA ROE Debt/Equity Dividend Yield + more
Coverage at launch

SMI (20 companies) + selected SPI mid caps. Full SPI coverage planned after data quality validation.

Historical depth

Targeting up to 30 years of historical fundamentals, subject to data quality validation. Normalised across fiscal year conventions.

Future access layer under exploration — MCP / AI-assisted workflows for querying verified Swiss financial data. This is exploratory and not part of the initial MVP.
Who it is for

Built for people who work
with Swiss equities seriously.

Analysts

Small buy-side teams and independent researchers who need clean data without maintaining their own pipeline.

Quants & Devs

Finance developers who need SIX fundamentals in a DataFrame, not a PDF. Python-native, no brittle scraper.

Fintech builders

Early-stage products that need Swiss equity data to move fast. No direct enterprise data vendor contract. No heavy procurement process.

Researchers

Finance professors and academic labs building factor models or panel studies on Swiss listed companies.

Not built for

Large banks and institutions with existing Bloomberg or Refinitiv access. Highly technical quants who prefer building their own pipeline from scratch. Family offices with heavy FINMA compliance requirements.

Credibility

Built on an existing
open-source foundation.

swiss-finance-data on PyPI

Open-source Python package published on PyPI, covering Swiss macro and market data. It has generated organic interest from finance professors and fintech developers, and serves as the technical foundation for this broader Swiss financial data project.
